About Teaching Torch

Empowering Sri Lankan Education

Our Mission

Teaching Torch is dedicated to providing free, high-quality educational resources to Sri Lankan students from Grade 6 to Advanced Level.

We believe that education should be accessible to all students, regardless of their economic background. Our platform offers comprehensive study materials, practice papers, and learning resources aligned with the Sri Lankan curriculum.

Our Values

Comprehensive Resources

Complete study materials for all subjects across grades 6-13, including textbooks, past papers, short notes, and video lessons.

Free Access

All resources are completely free to download and use. Education should never be limited by financial constraints.

Exam Excellence

Materials specifically designed to help students excel in their O/L and A/L examinations and beyond.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's premier digital education platform, bridging the gap between quality education and accessibility. We envision a future where every student has the tools they need to succeed, regardless of their location or circumstances.
